3 C> SUBPROGRAM: W3FB04 LATITUDE, LONGITUDE TO GRID COORDINATES
4 C> AUTHOR: MCDONELL,J. ORG: W345 DATE: 86-07-17
5 C>
6 C> ABSTRACT: CONVERTS THE COORDINATES OF A LOCATION ON EARTH FROM THE
7 C> NATURAL COORDINATE SYSTEM OF LATITUDE/LONGITUDE TO THE GRID (I,J)
8 C> COORDINATE SYSTEM OVERLAID ON A POLAR STEREOGRAPHIC MAP PRO-
9 C> JECTION TRUE AT 60 DEGREES N OR S LATITUDE. W3FB04 IS THE REVERSE
10 C> OF W3FB05.
11 C>
12 C> PROGRAM HISTORY LOG:
13 C> 86-07-17 MCDONELL,J.
14 C> 88-06-07 R.E.JONES CLEAN UP CODE, TAKE OUT GOTO, USE THEN, ELSE
15 C> 89-11-02 R.E.JONES CHANGE TO CRAY CFT77 FORTRAN
16 C>
17 C> USAGE: CALL W3FB04 (ALAT, ALONG, XMESHL, ORIENT, XI, XJ)
18 C>
19 C> INPUT VARIABLES:
20 C> NAMES INTERFACE DESCRIPTION OF VARIABLES AND TYPES
21 C> ------ --------- -----------------------------------------------
22 C> ALAT ARG LIST LATITUDE IN DEGREES (<0 IF SH)
23 C> ALONG ARG LIST WEST LONGITUDE IN DEGREES
24 C> XMESHL ARG LIST MESH LENGTH OF GRID IN KM AT 60 DEG LAT(<0 IF SH)
25 C> (190.5 LFM GRID, 381.0 NH PE GRID,-381.0 SH PE GRID)
26 C> ORIENT ARG LIST ORIENTATION WEST LONGITUDE OF THE GRID
27 C> (105.0 LFM GRID, 80.0 NH PE GRID, 260.0 SH PE GRID)
28 C>
29 C> OUTPUT VARIABLES:
30 C> NAMES INTERFACE DESCRIPTION OF VARIABLES AND TYPES
31 C> ------ --------- -----------------------------------------------
32 C> XI ARG LIST I OF THE POINT RELATIVE TO NORTH OR SOUTH POLE
33 C> XJ ARG LIST J OF THE POINT RELATIVE TO NORTH OR SOUTH POLE
34 C>
35 C> SUBPROGRAMS CALLED:
36 C> NAMES LIBRARY
37 C> ------------------------------------------------------- --------
38 C> COS SIN SYSLIB
39 C>
40 C> REMARKS: ALL PARAMETERS IN THE CALLING STATEMENT MUST BE
41 C> REAL. THE RANGE OF ALLOWABLE LATITUDES IS FROM A POLE TO
42 C> 30 DEGREES INTO THE OPPOSITE HEMISPHERE.
43 C> THE GRID USED IN THIS SUBROUTINE HAS ITS ORIGIN (I=0,J=0)
44 C> AT THE POLE IN EITHER HEMISPHERE, SO IF THE USER'S GRID HAS ITS
45 C> ORIGIN AT A POINT OTHER THAN THE POLE, A TRANSLATION IS NEEDED
46 C> TO GET I AND J. THE GRIDLINES OF I=CONSTANT ARE PARALLEL TO A
47 C> LONGITUDE DESIGNATED BY THE USER. THE EARTH'S RADIUS IS TAKEN
48 C> TO BE 6371.2 KM.
49 C>
50 C> WARNING: THIS CODE IS NOT VECTORIZED. TO VECTORIZE TAKE IT AND
51 C> SUBROUTINE IT CALLS AND PU THEM IN LINE.
52 C>
53 C> ATTRIBUTES:
54 C> LANGUAGE: CRAY CFT77 FORTRAN
55 C> MACHINE: CRAY Y-MP8/832
56 C>
57  SUBROUTINE w3fb04(ALAT,ALONG,XMESHL,ORIENT,XI,XJ)
58 C
59  DATA radpd /.01745329/
60  DATA earthr/6371.2/
61 C
62  re = (earthr * 1.86603) / xmeshl
63  xlat = alat * radpd
64 C
65  IF (xmeshl.GE.0.) THEN
66  wlong = (along + 180.0 - orient) * radpd
67  r = (re * cos(xlat)) / (1.0 + sin(xlat))
68  xi = r * sin(wlong)
69  xj = r * cos(wlong)
70  ELSE
71  re = -re
72  xlat = -xlat
73  wlong = (along - orient) * radpd
74  r = (re * cos(xlat)) / (1.0 + sin(xlat))
75  xi = r * sin(wlong)
76  xj = -r * cos(wlong)
77  ENDIF
78 C
79  RETURN
80  END
